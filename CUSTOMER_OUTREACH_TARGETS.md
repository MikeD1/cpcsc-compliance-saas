# ComplianceOne Customer Outreach Targets

Ontario/GTA-first outreach list for CPCSC Level 1 readiness validation.

Goal: book 10-15 discovery conversations and identify 3-5 design partners. The ask is feedback, not a cold purchase.

## Positioning

ComplianceOne helps Canadian defence suppliers turn CPCSC Level 1 preparation into practical, buyer-ready proof: organized evidence, control mapping, readiness gaps, ownership, and a clean report for customer/procurement/security conversations.

Use:

- CPCSC Level 1 readiness
- buyer-ready proof
- evidence organization
- readiness snapshot
- designed to expand once official Level 2/3 requirements are available and reviewed

Avoid:

- certification
- government-approved
- guaranteed compliance
- Level 2/3 ready today
- replacing consultants, assessors, auditors, or legal advice

## Discovery questions

- Where does CPCSC/security-readiness documentation live today: spreadsheet, folder, Word doc, consultant report, email, or someone’s head?
- Who owns keeping it current?
- What usually triggers the work: customer question, bid, prime requirement, internal leadership, renewal, audit, or consultant recommendation?
- Do you know what “good enough” evidence looks like for each control?
- What part is most painful: knowing what to do, finding evidence, assigning owners, keeping it current, or producing something shareable?
- Would a buyer-ready readiness PDF be useful, or would buyers expect something different?
- Would this replace/improve the current spreadsheet or document process?
- What would need to be true before you would pay for it?
