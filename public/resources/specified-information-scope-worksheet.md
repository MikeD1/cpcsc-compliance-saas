# Specified Information (SI) Scope Worksheet

Use this worksheet to identify where federal Specified Information (SI) exists so the organization can apply CPCSC Level 1 controls to the right systems, people, devices, tools, and locations.

SI is sensitive, non-classified Government of Canada information that must be protected when handled, processed, or stored by non-Government of Canada organizations.
